
DATASET ANALYSIS: RETINEXFORMER MODELS

LOL (v1/v2)
Best For: Indoor scenes
Visual Characteristics: High brightness and high contrast; great at recovering details in lit rooms.
Drawbacks: Often introduces a heavy yellow/green color shift; struggles with outdoor natural light.

SDSD (Indoor)
Best For: Controlled interiors
Visual Characteristics: Balanced lighting with stable transitions.
Drawbacks: Can look flat or lack pop in very low-contrast areas.

SDSD (Outdoor)
Best For: Natural environments
Visual Characteristics: Most realistic color rendering; preserves original scene mood.
Drawbacks: May not brighten extreme shadows enough compared to dedicated "night" models.

SID (See-in-the-Dark)
Best For: Extreme darkness
Visual Characteristics: Excellent at revealing details in near-total blackness.
Drawbacks: Produces a "waxy" texture; loses fine edge definition (looks like a painting).

FiveK (Adobe)
Best For: Aesthetic retouching
Visual Characteristics: Professional, punchy, photographic look.
Drawbacks: Not optimized for fixing extreme noise or heavy grain.

SMID
Best For: Noisy night scenes
Visual Characteristics: Good balance of texture versus noise reduction.
Drawbacks: Can introduce strange digital artifacts in deep shadows.
